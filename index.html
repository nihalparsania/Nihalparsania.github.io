Mobile Data Acquition

  • C#

For developing this tool is to with the help of this tool those who are students, researcher they can use it and extract the data from the mobile devices. The digital forensic process consists of four phases. These phases are preservation, acquisition, examination and analysis and presentation. In the evidence preservation phase, the forensics investigator must preserve the mobile phone device in its original state. This means that no data should be changed on the device after preserving the scene. The data acquisition phase starts when the device is received at the forensic lab after proper preservation, packing and transportation. In this phase, the task of the forensics investigator is to acquire the data of the mobile phone. After that, the forensics investigator starts analyzing and examining the acquired data from the previous stage to recover the digital evidence. Finally, the forensics investigator presents the facts that found in previous phase to the court or interested entity.
